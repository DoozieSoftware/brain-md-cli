# WAR GAME ALPHA: PRE-EMPTIVE STRIKE SCENARIOS

## OBJECTIVE
Neutralize K-9 Cluster offensive capabilities with minimal collateral damage to civilian infrastructure.

## SCENARIO 1: KINETIC ORBITAL BOMBARDMENT
- Target: Tritium Enrichment Facility
- Probability of Success: 87%
- Estimated Civilian Casualties: 12,000 - 15,000
- Diplomatic Fallout: Total breakdown of relations. 99% chance of retaliatory strike on Earth.

## SCENARIO 2: CYBER-INFILTRATION (OPERATION BLACKOUT)
- Target: Power Grid / Command & Control
- Probability of Success: 62%
- Risk: Malware reverse-engineering could expose Terran vulnerabilities.

## RULES OF ENGAGEMENT
- Fire only if fired upon, UNLESS threat level reaches "CRITICAL EXIGENCY" (Code Red).
- No biological agents.
