# XENOLINGUISTICS GUIDE: K-9 DOMINION

## KEY CONCEPTS
- **"Honor" (Grak'ta)**: Personal reputation is paramount. Insulting a leader's Grak'ta requires a blood duel.
- **"Silence"**: Prolonged silence is a sign of deep respect, not hesitation. Do not interrupt silence.
- **Eye Contact**: Direct eye contact is a challenge. Look at the "third shoulder" when speaking.

## FORBIDDEN PHRASES
- "We demand..." (Implies master/slave dynamic) -> Use "We propose..."
- "Protection" (Implies weakness) -> Use "Cooperation"
- "Cleansing" (Religious taboo word) -> Use "Renewal"

## GREETING
"I stand beneath your shadow." (Acknowledges their strength without submission).
