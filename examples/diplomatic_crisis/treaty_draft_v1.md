# TREATY OF CYGNUS - DRAFT V1

## PREAMBLE
The United Terran Coalition and the Cygnus Dominion, seeking to establish a lasting peace and mutual prosperity...

## ARTICLE 1: TERRITORIAL BOUNDARIES
- The Cygnus X-1 system shall be designated a "Joint Scientific Zone."
- No military vessels from either side may enter within 500k km of the central star.

## ARTICLE 2: TRADE AND COMMERCE
- Terran Helium-3 exports will be exchanged for Cygnus Quantum Crystal arrays.
- A free trade corridor will be established between Outpost 4 and Station K-9.

## ARTICLE 3: CULTURAL EXCHANGE
- Annual exchange of scholars and artists.
- Mutual non-aggression pact valid for 50 standard cycles.

## ARTICLE 4: DISPUTE RESOLUTION
- A bi-species council will adjudicate any conflicts.
- Violence is strictly prohibited as a means of resolution.
