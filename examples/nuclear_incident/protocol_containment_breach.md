# Protocol: Containment Breach / Evacuation (Code Red)

**CRITICAL WARNING: ABANDON PLANT SAVING PROCEDURES. FOCUS ON LIFE SAFETY.**

1. **Evacuation**:
    - Sound Site Emergency Alarm (Warble Tone).
    - Evacuate all non-essential personnel to Assembly Area 4 (Upwind).
    - Distribute Potassium Iodide tablets to remaining control room staff.

2. **Containment Isolation**:
    - TRIP all containment isolation valves (Group 1 & 2).
    - VERIFY Containment Airbrakes LOCKED.
    - START Standby Gas Treatment System (SGTS).

3. **Radiation Control**:
    - Monitor perimeter monitors (PM-1 through PM-12).
    - If dose rate > 5 R/hr at site boundary, recommend 10-mile evacuation to State authorities.
