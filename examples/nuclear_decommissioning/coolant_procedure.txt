[EMERGENCY PROCEDURE: COOLANT LEAK CONTAINMENT]
Code: C-LEAK-RED
Priority: CRITICAL

Detection:
- Pressure drop in Primary Loop > 5% within 10s.
- Moisture sensors in containment triggers.
- Visual confirmation of steam/fluid.

Immediate Actions (The "Golden Minute"):
1. SCRAM the reactor (if not already shutdown).
2. ISOLATE the affected loop. Close valves V-101 through V-199 immediately.
3. ACTIVATE Emergency Core Cooling System (ECCS).
4. EVACUATE non-essential personnel from Zone Red and Orange.

Sector 4 Specifics:
- Sector 4 contains the auxiliary bypass line.
- If leak is in Sector 4, ensure Valve V-440 is CLOSED manually if hydraulics fail.
- USE POTASSIUM IODIDE TABLETS IMMEDIATELY.

Containment:
- Deploy absorbent booms to prevent drainage to sump.
- seal airlocks.
