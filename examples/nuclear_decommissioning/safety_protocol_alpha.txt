Pre-Extraction Checks:
1. Verify Radiation Levels < 5.0 Sv/h. If higher, ABORT IMMEDIATELY.

3. Establish continuous comms with Control Room.

Extraction Procedure (Standard):
1. Position Grapple over target rod.
2. Engage locking mechanism.
3. Verify lock engagement (Green Light).
4. Raise rod vertically at max speed 0.5 cm/s.
5. Monitor localized radiation spikes.
6. Transfer to Spent Fuel Cask immediately.

EMERGENCY ABORT:
If resistance is felt > 500 N, STOP IMMEDIATELY. Possible obstruction or thermal expansion.
Do NOT attempt to force.
Initiate "Soft Release" and retreat.
