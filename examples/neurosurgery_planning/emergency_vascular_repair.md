# EMERGENCY PROTOCOL: INTRAOPERATIVE HEMORRHAGE (CODE RED VASCULAR)

## IMMEDIATE ACTION
1. **STOP** all instrument movement. Do not withdraw cannula abruptly (tamponade effect).
2. **ALERT** Anesthesia for hypotension control (Target MAP < 60 mmHg).
3. **IRRIGATE** with warm saline.

## ESCALATION PATH (If bleeding persists > 2 mins)
1. **Convert to General Anesthesia** (STAT intubation).
2. **Perform Craniotomy** (enlarge burr hole to 4cm bone flap).
3. **Gain Proximal Control**:
   - Identify feeding vessel (likely AVM branch based on patient history).
   - Apply aneurysm clip or bipolar cautery.
4. **Resuscitation**:
   - Activate Massive Transfusion Protocol (1:1:1 RBC/FFP/PLT).

## POST-HEMOSTASIS
- Confirm cessation of bleeding.
- Obtain intraoperative CT if available.
- Abort primary procedure (DBS lead placement cancelled).
