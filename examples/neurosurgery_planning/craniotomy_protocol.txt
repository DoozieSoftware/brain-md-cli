STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: STEREOTACTIC BIOPSY / ELECTRODE PLACEMENT

1. PRE-OP CHECKLIST
   - Verify patient ID and laterality (LEFT side).
   - Load trajectory plan into StealthStation.
   - Confirm antibiotic prophylaxis administered.

2. ANESTHESIA
   - Conscious sedation (MAC) preferred for electrode testing.
   - Maintain SBP < 140 mmHg.

3. APPROACH
   - Pre-coronal burr hole (Kocher's point).
   - Incise dura (cruciate fashion).
   - Secure stereotactic arc.

4. TRAJECTORY VERIFICATION
   - Insert micro-drive.
   - Advance recording electrode to 10mm above target.
   - Record MER (Micro-Electrode Recording) activity.

5. FINAL PLACEMENT
   - Confirm VIM thalamic signature (burst cells).
   - Test stimulation for tremor suppression.
   - Lock lead.
