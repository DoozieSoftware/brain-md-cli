# MOI BURN PROTOCOL ALPHA

## OBJECTIVE
Mars Orbital Insertion (MOI) to achieve a polar science orbit.

## PARAMETERS
- Target Delta-V: 1200 m/s
- Burn Duration: 14 minutes
- Orientation: Retrograde
- Engine: Main Thruster (Bell nozzle)

## CRITICAL CHECKS
1. Verify Solar Arrays are STOWED to prevent structural damage from g-forces.
2. Verify communication link with Deep Space Network (DSN).
3. Confirm telemetry matches predictive models within 0.1% tolerance.

## ABORT CONDITIONS
- Engine chamber pressure < 90% nominal.
- Gyroscope drift > 0.5 degrees.
- Unexpected debris radar contact.
