# HAZOP GUIDELINES: REACTOR UNIT

## Node 1: Reactor Vessel

### Guide Word: HIGH PRESSURE
*   **Cause**: Runaway reaction, blocked discharge, external fire.
*   **Consequence**: Vessel rupture, release of toxic gas.
*   **Safeguard**: PSV-101 (Relief Valve) set to 10% above max operating pressure.
*   **Action**: Verify relief sizing for worst-case scenario (fire case).

### Guide Word: HIGH TEMPERATURE
*   **Cause**: Loss of cooling water, catalyst overdose.
*   **Consequence**: Polymer degradation, pressure spike.
*   **Safeguard**: ESD (Emergency Shutdown) triggers at 175 C.
