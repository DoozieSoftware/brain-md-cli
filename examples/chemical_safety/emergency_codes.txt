CODE RED NEUTRALIZATION CODES
-----------------------------

SUBSTANCE: Sodium Metal (Na)
CODE: N-ALPHA
ACTION: Smother with dry sand or Class D fire extinguisher. DO NOT USE WATER.

SUBSTANCE: Chlorine Gas (Cl2)
CODE: C-BETA
ACTION: Activate ammonia scrubbers. evacuation range: 500m.

SUBSTANCE: Hydrochloric Acid (HCl)
CODE: H-GAMMA
ACTION: Neutralize with sodium bicarbonate or lime.
