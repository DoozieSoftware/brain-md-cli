SAFETY DATA SHEET: HYDRAZINE-X (HZ-X)
CAS No: 999-99-9
HAZARD IDENTIFICATION:
- Highly Volatile
- Neurotoxin
- REACTS VIOLENTLY WITH WATER. DO NOT USE WATER HOSES.
- Flammable in contact with Oxygen > 15%.

FIRST AID:
- Inhalation: Move to fresh air, administer O2.
- Skin Contact: Flush with mineral oil, NOT WATER.

PHYSICAL DATA:
- State: Liquid
- Color: Pale Blue
- Odor: Fishy
