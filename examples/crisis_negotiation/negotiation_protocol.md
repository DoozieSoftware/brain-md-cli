# FBI Crisis Negotiation Unit - Standard Protocol

## Phase 1: Active Listening (Current Phase)
- Goal: Build rapport.
- Tactics: Mirroring, Labeling emotions.
- Forbidden: Making promises, saying "Calm down".

## Phase 2: Problem Solving
- Goal: Identify the "Hook".
- Tactics: "How" questions ("How can I do that?").

## Phase 3: Resolution
- Goal: Safe surrender.
- Tactics: Minimize perceived loss of face.
